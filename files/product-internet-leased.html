Enterprise-Grade Internet Connectivity

Our Internet Leased Line service provides your business with a dedicated, high-capacity internet connection that isn't shared with other users. Unlike standard broadband connections, leased lines offer symmetrical upload and download speeds, making them ideal for businesses that rely heavily on cloud services, VoIP communications, or need to transfer large files regularly.

With guaranteed bandwidth and uptime backed by rigorous Service Level Agreements (SLAs), you can be confident your critical applications will always have the connectivity they need. Our redundant network architecture ensures maximum reliability, with automatic failover to backup routes in case of any disruption.

Business Applications

Internet Leased Lines are perfect for organizations that:

  • Host services such as web servers, email, or e-commerce platforms
  • Run VoIP or video conferencing systems
  • Need to connect multiple office locations securely
  • Transfer large files regularly
  • Require guaranteed uptime with financial compensation for any outages

What is the difference between a leased line and regular business broadband?

Unlike standard broadband connections that are shared among many users (contended), a leased line provides dedicated bandwidth exclusively for your business. Leased lines offer symmetrical speeds (same upload and download), guaranteed bandwidth at all times, much lower latency, and come with strict SLAs for uptime and support response times. While more expensive than broadband, they provide enterprise-grade reliability essential for business-critical applications.

How long does installation take?

Typical installation times range from 2-6 weeks depending on various factors including your location, existing infrastructure, and any wayleave permissions required. Urban locations with existing fiber infrastructure nearby generally have faster installation times. During the quotation process, we can provide a more accurate timeline for your specific installation.

What happens if there's an outage?

Our network is monitored 24/7/365 by our Network Operations Center, which proactively identifies and addresses issues often before they affect your service. In the event of an outage, our SLA guarantees a response within 15 minutes and an engineer dispatch within 2 hours if required. If we fail to restore service within the SLA timeframe, you receive service credits as compensation. For mission-critical applications, we also offer dual-carrier resilient connections that provide automatic failover in case of issues with the primary connection.

Can I upgrade bandwidth later if my needs change?

Absolutely. One of the key advantages of our leased line service is scalability. In most cases, bandwidth upgrades can be implemented remotely with no additional hardware changes required, often within 24-48 hours of request. This allows your connection to grow with your business without the need for disruptive re-installations. We offer flexible contracts that allow for bandwidth upgrades at any time.

Do you provide the router and other equipment needed?

Yes, our service includes all necessary equipment including a carrier-grade router configured to your requirements. The equipment remains our property and is fully managed and supported as part of your service. This includes firmware updates, configuration changes, and replacement in case of hardware failure. For specialized requirements, we can also work with your existing enterprise equipment subject to compatibility assessment.
