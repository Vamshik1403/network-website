What happens during a power outage?

Our door lock systems include backup battery power that activates automatically during outages, ensuring continuous operation for up to 24 hours. Additionally, there's always a mechanical key override for emergency access.

Can I integrate this with my existing security system?

Yes, our door lock access systems are designed to integrate with most major security platforms, including alarm systems, CCTV cameras, and building management software through standard protocols.

How secure is the wireless connectivity?

All wireless communications use advanced AES-256 encryption, the same level used by financial institutions. Additionally, our systems employ multiple authentication layers to prevent unauthorized access attempts.

Can I add more doors to the system later?

Absolutely. Our systems are modular and scalable, allowing you to start with a single door and expand to hundreds if needed. The control software is designed to handle multiple access points without requiring a complete system overhaul.

What maintenance is required?

Our door lock systems require minimal maintenance. We recommend a professional inspection once a year to check mechanical components, update software if needed, and replace backup batteries. Most systems will alert you when maintenance is required.
