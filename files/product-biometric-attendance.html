How accurate is fingerprint recognition?

Our biometric scanners have an accuracy rate of 99.9% with a false acceptance rate of less than 0.0001%. The system uses advanced algorithms to identify unique fingerprint patterns, ensuring reliable identification even with minor cuts or abrasions.

Can it integrate with our existing HR system?

Yes, our system is designed with open APIs that enable seamless integration with most popular HR and payroll systems. We provide pre-built connectors for major platforms like SAP, Workday, and ADP, along with custom integration services for proprietary systems.

How does the system handle power outages?

All our biometric terminals include built-in backup batteries that provide up to 4 hours of continuous operation during power outages. The system also stores attendance data locally before synchronizing with the central database, ensuring no check-ins are lost during connectivity issues.

Is employee data kept secure and private?

Absolutely. Our system is designed with privacy and security as top priorities. Biometric data is encrypted using AES-256 standards and never stored as actual fingerprint images, but rather as mathematical templates that cannot be reverse-engineered. We are fully compliant with GDPR and other global privacy regulations.

What kind of reports can the system generate?

The system includes over 50 pre-built reports including daily attendance summaries, late arrivals, early departures, overtime, absences, and department-wise attendance statistics. You can also create custom reports using our intuitive report builder, export data in multiple formats (PDF, Excel, CSV), and schedule automated report delivery to stakeholders.
